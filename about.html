About Me

I’m Janice Zhang, a third-year Computer Engineering student at UBC with hands-on experience across hardware, software, and design. I enjoy solving problems that blend technical depth with creative implementation.

At Formula UBC Racing, I lead the geometry design for our 2025 race car, integrating CAD models and physical components across subsystems. My work includes developing simulation workflows, refining mechanical fit, and collaborating closely with other teams to achieve high performance and precision.

Beyond mechanical design, I’ve developed projects in embedded systems and AI, including a real-time subtitle and translation application, a reinforcement learning sandbox built from scratch, and a hardware-driven water simulation using ESP32 sensors.

I’m particularly interested in roles that connect engineering design, software systems, and intelligent automation. My goal is to build efficient, purposeful systems that bring ideas into motion.
